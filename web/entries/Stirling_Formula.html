Stirling's formula

 

Title: Stirling's formula
Author: Manuel Eberl
Submission date: 2016-09-01
Abstract: This work contains a proof of Stirling's formula both for the factorial n! ∼ √2πn (n/e)n on natural numbers and the real Gamma function Γ(x) ∼ √2π/x (x/e)x. The proof is based on work by Graham Jameson.
