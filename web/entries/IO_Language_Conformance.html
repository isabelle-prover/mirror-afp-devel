Conformance Relations between Input/Output Languages

Robert Sachtleben ðŸ“§

September 1, 2023

Abstract

This entry formalises the paper of the same name by Huang et al. and presents a unifying characterisation of well-known conformance relations such as equivalence and language inclusion (reduction) on languages over input/output pairs. This characterisation simplifies comparisons between conformance relations and from it a fundamental necessary and sufficient criterion for conformance testing is developed.
