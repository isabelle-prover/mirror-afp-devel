Abstract

This entry contains formalization of differential privacy in a general setting, based on the seminal textbook of Dwork and Roth and the papers of statistical divergence for differential privacy [Sato and Katsumata, MSCS2023 and Barthe and Olmedo,ICALP2013].

This entry includes the following formalization:

  • Measurable space of lists and measurability of list operations
  • Laplace distribution
  • The statisatical divergence for differential privacy
  • Differential privacy and its basic properties
  • Randomized Response
  • Laplace mechanism
  • The report noisy max mechanism
