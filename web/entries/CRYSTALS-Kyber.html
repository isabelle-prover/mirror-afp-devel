Abstract

This article formalizes the specification and the algorithm of the cryptographic scheme CRYSTALS-KYBER with multiplication using the Number Theoretic Transform and verifies its (1-Î´)-correctness proof. CRYSTALS-KYBER is a key encapsulation mechanism in lattice-based post-quantum cryptography. This entry formalizes the key generation, encryption and decryption algorithms and shows that the algorithm decodes correctly under a highly probable assumption ((1-Î´)-correctness). Moreover, the Number Theoretic Transform (NTT) in the case of Kyber and the convolution theorem thereon is formalized.
