Lebesgue-Stieltjes Integral

Yosuke Ito 📧

March 4, 2026

Abstract

This entry formalizes some basic facts and lemmas relating to the integration with respect to the Lebesgue-Stieltjes measure (interval measure). It includes the well-known formula to calculate the Lebesgue-Stieltjes integral: \[ \int g(x) \, dF(x) = \int g(x) F'(x) \, dx. \]
