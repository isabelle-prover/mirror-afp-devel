The LambdaMu-Calculus

Cristina Matache , Victor B. F. Gomes and Dominic P. Mulligan

August 16, 2017

Abstract

The propositions-as-types correspondence is ordinarily presented as linking the metatheory of typed 位-calculi and the proof theory of intuitionistic logic. Griffin observed that this correspondence could be extended to classical logic through the use of control operators. This observation set off a flurry of further research, leading to the development of Parigots 位渭-calculus. In this work, we formalise 位渭- calculus in Isabelle/HOL and prove several metatheoretical properties such as type preservation and progress.
