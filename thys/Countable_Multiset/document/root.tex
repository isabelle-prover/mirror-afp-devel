\title{Formalization of Countable Multisets}
\author{Mathias Schack Rabing \and Dmitriy Traytel}

\begin{document}

\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
  We define \emph{countable multisets} as a generalization of finite multisets.
  Countable multisets are equivalently represented as functions from elements to extended natural numbers
  ($\mathbb{N} \cup \{\infty\}$) returning non-zero values for countably many elements,
  or as quotients of lazy lists modulo infinitary permutations.
  We register countable multisets as a bounded natural functor (BNF), enabling nested (co)recursion through them
  in (co)datatypes. We further define the subtype of
  \emph{countably infinite multisets} and register it as a BNF, too.
\end{abstract}
