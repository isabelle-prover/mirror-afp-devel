\title{Differential Privacy}
\author{Tetsuya Sato \and Yasuhiko Minamide}
\maketitle

\abstract{
This entry contains formalization of differential privacy in a general setting,
based on the seminal textbook of Dwork and Roth~\cite{DworkRothTCS-042}
and the papers of statistical divergence for differential privacy~\cite{BartheOlmedo2013,Sato_Katsumata_2023}.

This entry includes the following formalization:
\begin{itemize}
\item Measurable space of lists and measurability of list operations,
\item Laplace distribution,
\item The statisatical divergence for differential privacy,
\item Differential privacy and its basic properties,
\item Randomized Response,
\item Laplace mechanism,
\item The report noisy max mechanism.
\end{itemize}
}
