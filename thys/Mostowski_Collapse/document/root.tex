\title{The Mostowski Collapse Theorem}
\author{Arthur Freitas Ramos\\
David Barros Hulak\\
Ruy J. G. B. de Queiroz}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
This entry formalizes the Mostowski collapse theorem in Isabelle/ZF
\cite{Mostowski1949,Mostowski1969}.  For a set equipped with a well-founded
extensional relation, the development defines the collapsing map by
well-founded recursion, proves that its range is transitive, and shows that the
map is an order isomorphism between the original relation and membership on the
transitive range.  The construction is also proved unique among maps satisfying
the same recursive equation.  The result is a standard bridge from abstract
well-founded extensional structures to transitive membership structures, and
complements the existing Isabelle/ZF and AFP developments on ordinals,
cardinals, forcing, and transitive models \cite{Kunen1980,Jech2003}.  AI
assistance was used for proof engineering.  The final definitions, statements,
and proofs are checked by Isabelle.
\end{abstract}
