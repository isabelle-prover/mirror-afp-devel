\title{Regular Sets}
\author{Alexander Krauss and Tobias Nipkow}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
This is a library of constructions on languages, i.e.\ sets of words, i.e.\
lists. It provides the regular operations of concatenation, iteration,
Kleene star and derivative. A datatype of regular expressions and a mapping
to regular sets is also given.
\end{abstract}
